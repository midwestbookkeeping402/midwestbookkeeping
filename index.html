Entity Education

Structures Simplified

Choosing the right structure is a two-part decision: How you want to be protected **legally**, and how you want to be taxed by the **IRS**.

1

Corporate & Legal Entities

Legal Structure Formation Legal Protection Summary
Sole Proprietorship Automatic None You and the business are the same person. If the business is sued, you are personally sued.
LLC State Filing High A "Legal Shield." It separates your house and car from your business risks.
Corporation State Filing High A separate "Legal Person." Essential for raising money from outside investors.
2

Tax Classifications

Tax Status Tax Method Key Advantage Summary
Pass-Through Individual Return Simple Filing Business profit "passes through" to your personal tax return.
S-Corp Self-Employment Cap Tax Savings A way to lower your Social Security/Medicare taxes as an owner.
C-Corp Entity Level Tax Investor Friendly The company pays its own taxes. Best for very large growth plans.

The "Busy Season" Reality Check

Why your CPA isn't your bookkeeper

A common misconception is that your CPA will "fix the books" during tax season. In reality, CPAs are high-level tax strategists, not data entry clerks.

The CPA's Focus

During busy season, a CPA is managing hundreds of deadlines. They do not have time to go back 12 months and categorize thousands of bank transactions.

The "Tax-Ready" Gap

If your bookkeeping is messy, a CPA will either charge a massive cleanup premium or file based on potentially inaccurate data‚Äîincreasing your audit risk.
