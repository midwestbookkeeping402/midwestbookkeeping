Bookkeeper vs. Accountant

The Bookkeeper

"The Financial Architect"

A bookkeeper is responsible for the daily recording of all financial transactions. They ensure every dollar is accounted for and your ledgers are perfectly organized.

    The Accountant

    "The Strategic Analyst"

    An accountant uses the data recorded by the bookkeeper to provide high-level analysis, ensure IRS compliance, and perform strategic tax planning for your business growth.
