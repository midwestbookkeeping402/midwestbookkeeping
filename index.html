Entity Education

Structures Simplified

Choosing the right structure is a two-part decision: How you want to be protected **legally**, and how you want to be taxed by the **IRS**.

1

Corporate & Legal Entities Focus: Legal protection and ownership.

Legal Structure Formation Legal Protection Summary
Sole Proprietorship Automatic None You and the business are the same. Personal assets are at risk in legal matters.
LLC State Filing High A "Legal Shield." It separates your home and car from your business risks.
Corporation State Filing High A separate "Legal Person." Essential for raising money from outside investors.
2

Tax Classifications Focus: How much you pay the IRS.

Tax Status Tax Method Key Advantage Summary
Pass-Through Individual Return Simple Filing Business profit flows directly to your personal tax return.
S-Corp Self-Employment Cap Tax Savings A method to lower your Social Security/Medicare taxes as an owner.
C-Corp Entity Level Tax Investor Friendly The company pays its own taxes. Best for large scale Midwest ventures.

During busy season, a CPA manages hundreds of deadlines. They don't have time for a 12-month transaction cleanup.
